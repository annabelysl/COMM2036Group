Examples of Disinformation

One case of disinformation involved Bill gates in the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic. The most recent high-profile case of disinformation has been that of Bill Gates. Some theories were: Bill Gates has full knowledge of the virus. He engineered the virus and the pandemic in order to benefit from the enormous profits of producing and selling vaccines. Furthermore, Bill Gates desires to develop a microchip for billions of vaccinated people to track their activities.

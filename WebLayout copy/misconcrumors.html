Common Misconceptions about Rumors

The misconception of rumors
Rumours and gossip are both results of human interaction, and they are the two things that most people usually mix up. However, there are differences between rumours and gossip. Rumour refers to spreading some information about someone or something without confirmation, and it can be true or false, while gossip refers to just discussing someone or something with others.

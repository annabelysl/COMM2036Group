Overview

rumors

a piece of information, a statement, or an opinion without confirmation that is disseminated from person to person.

fake news

news reported with no discernible sources and
verified content; a story created by someone on purpose to
deceive the readers, it might include a false message and data.

Misinformation

false or inaccurate information. For example, false
rumours, insults, hoaxes, and more deliberate misinformation
includes misinformation like pranks, phishing attacks, and propaganda.

Disinformation

the deliberate spreading of false news or
unconfirmed information to deceive people.

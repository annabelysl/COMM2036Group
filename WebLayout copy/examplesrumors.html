The Tesla Rumor

Example case in real life
A Tesla Model Y was involved in a serious car accident in Chaozhou City, Guangdong Province on November 5, killing a motorcycle driver and a female high school student on the spot, and injuring three others.The report quoted the son of the 55-year-old man driver as saying that his father tried to brake in front of his shop but failed. Tesla told Reuters that the Chinese police are seeking third-party appraisal agencies to participate in the investigation to find out the truth of the accident. The company will actively provide necessary support and appeal to the public not to listen to rumours. To check out more about this news, please press this link
